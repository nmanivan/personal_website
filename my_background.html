About Me

My name is Narayan Manivannan, and I'm currently a Sophomore studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan College of Engineering. I was born in Portland, Oregon, then moved to Novi, Michigan, where I stayed for most of my life with my family. I was first introduced to Computer Science my junior year of high school, and my love for the field has only grown. I plan on working hard and learning as much as I can in school while also engaging in side projects and preparing for interviews. My dream job is to work for Facebook, as its culture is centered on trust and teamwork, which I believe suits me well. My primary goal is to lead a happy life in the future, and this will result from the passion and hard work I show in the present.
